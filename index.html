FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What exactly does CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) mean here?

You agree a fixed fee with us for every incremental sale we drive — say $12 for a recovered cart sale. If we drive 1,000 sales this month, you pay us $12,000. If we drive zero, you pay us zero. No media risk, no retainer, no platform subscription.

How do you prove the sales you claim are incremental?

Every campaign we run includes a holdout group — a randomized 10% of eligible users we deliberately don't target. We measure the conversion gap between the exposed group and the holdout and only bill on the lift. You also get row‑level match‑back to your order system so you can audit every claim.

Do I need to give you a dashboard login or integrate software?

No. We run everything on our end — media, creatives, exchanges, push infrastructure. You install one tag (takes under 30 minutes with your dev team) and share your product feed. That's the extent of your integration work.

Will this conflict with my existing paid media or affiliate partners?

No. We only retarget users who have already visited your site and didn't convert. We never bid on your brand keywords, we suppress customers who bought in the last N days, and we run alongside email, SMS and paid social, not against them.

How fast can we go live?

Most partner brands are running their first campaign within 14 days. The timeline depends on how quickly our creative team can produce your initial ad variants and on your legal/privacy review of the tag.

What categories won't you work with?

We don't run retargeting for adult, gambling, firearms, short‑term payday loans, or any regulated category we can't serve compliantly across the major ad exchanges. Everyone else is welcome.
