Before you ask

Questions we hear most often.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. The CPA model means you pay per recovered sale. If a programme doesn't perform, it doesn't cost you anything and you can walk.

How quickly can campaigns go live?

Typical time from signed proposal to first impressions is 7–10 business days. Pixel integration takes under half an hour; creative builds take the rest of the week.

How do you measure attribution?

We agree the attribution window upfront (usually 7‑day click, 1‑day view), deduplicate against your other paid channels, and expose sale‑level reporting so every invoice item can be traced back to an impression.

Do you take equity or retainers?

Neither. CPA only. If your model genuinely doesn't support a CPA price (B2B enterprise, long offline cycles), we'll tell you and suggest another partner.

Will you work with my existing agency?

Yes — most of our retail clients keep their paid search and Meta agency in place. We handle retargeting as a standalone channel with suppression agreements between us.

Which markets do you cover?

Programmatic display runs globally. Web push is concentrated in markets where we've built local subscriber graphs — SEA, Middle East, Western Europe and ANZ are strongest.
